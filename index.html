An Insider's Guide to NFYW

NFYW can be both amazingly fun & incredibly overwhelming, especially if you've never been. Luckily, I'm here to give you an insider's guide and make your first show a pleasurable experience. By taking any tips and tricks, and following your gut, you'll have an unforgettable experience!

Getting Tickets & Pricing the Shows

If you're lucky or connected you can get an invite, sans the price tag.

Dressing for the Shows

Always be true to your own sense of style, if you don't you'll be uncomfortable the whole time and it will show. Remember, NYFW is about expressing yourself and takig in what the designers have chose to express through their non lines. Also it's important to wear shoes you'll be comfortable in all day. Obviously you want to look good, but you'll be on your feet all day long, so be prepared.
